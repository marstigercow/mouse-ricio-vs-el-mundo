Mousericio - Capítulo 1

This is a small project where I tried to adapt Carlos Salazar Herrera's story into the style of a Japanese manga. Probably the biggest challenge was organizing the different panels to tell the story and writing Gabriel Sánchez's thoughts to convey what was happening in a way that felt faithful to the original tale, since it originally has an omniscient narrator, and the only dialogue in the entire story consists of 3 lines.
I tried to recreate the manga style, but the final artwork ended up being more of a mix between cartoon and my own style.
The entire project took me about a month. I'm happy with the result and plan to adapt more stories.
